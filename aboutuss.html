Healing A Hurting World.

What We Do

We at GOONJ, aim at working towards a world where every child has a happy, healthy and secure childhood we passionately believe they deserve.

India is only the second country where children live in extreme poverty. We are helping to change this by providing Health Nutrition, Education and Child Protection that benefits thousands of children every year.
